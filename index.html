How Our Plans Work

Our plans are based on the level of resource and priority we allocate to your business each month.

Rather than charging for a fixed number of hours, each plan gives your business access to a defined level of implementation capacity. Some months may require more work, while others may require less. Our focus is on completing the activities that move your business forward, not artificially spending time to meet a quota.

This means we are incentivised to achieve outcomes as efficiently as possible while ensuring sufficient capacity is available when more intensive work is required.

Every plan works toward the same destination — the difference is the level of monthly resource allocation, and therefore the likely speed of progress.
