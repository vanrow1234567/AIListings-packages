The programme is ongoing and delivered in rolling three-month review cycles. At each review we assess performance, priorities, opportunities and next steps. There is no fixed minimum total term; the programme continues on a rolling basis until cancelled.

Foundation Package

The Foundation Package is provided on recurring three (3) month review cycles. You may cancel by providing at least thirty (30) days' written notice before the end of your current three-month review cycle. If no notice is received, the Programme will automatically renew for a further three-month review cycle.

The Growth and Accelerator Packages are subject to an initial minimum term of three (3) months. To cancel at the end of the initial term, you must provide at least thirty (30) days' written notice before the expiry of the three-month period.

If no notice is received, the Programme will automatically continue on a rolling monthly basis. Following the initial term, either party may terminate the Programme by providing thirty (30) days' written notice.
